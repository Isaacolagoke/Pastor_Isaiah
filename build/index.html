Pastor. Husband. Father

Pastor Isaiah Oyedele is a passionate servant of God, devoted to inspiring and transforming lives through the Word of God. As the visionary leader of Chosen Church Ministries in Leeds, UK, he is committed to empowering individuals to fulfill their divine purpose and fostering a community rooted in faith, love, and service. With a heart for outreach and spiritual growth.
